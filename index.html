Medstop Checker

Our MedStop Checker automates the process of verifying driver drug test statuses through the official MedStop system. It ensures that you always have access to the most up-to-date information without manual tracking or delays.


  • Automatic and continuous status retrieval for all listed drivers
  • Real-time result delivery directly to your private Telegram channel
  • Color-coded highlighting of failed, pending, or problematic checks
  • Seamless integration into your hiring or compliance workflows
  • Scheduled checks every 15 minutes to ensure no updates are missed
  • Helps prevent violations and missed deadlines by acting proactively
  • Eliminates the need for manual login and status review
  • Improves compliance monitoring and reduces operational stress

This tool is ideal for fleets, dispatchers, safety managers, or anyone handling compliance. Save time, stay compliant, and always be one step ahead with MedStop Checker.
